The Dover-Sherborn robotics team, Broken Code, has been competeing in the FIRST Tech Challenge for over 10 years. Established in 2011 the team has been a FTC state finalist, as well as participated in mutiple FTC seasons. Throughout each competition season we provide D-S students with an opportunity to nurture their interest in science, technology, engineering and math as well as financing and media.
